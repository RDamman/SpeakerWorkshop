Volume Calculator dialog (Options Calculator Volume)

Use this dialog to calculate the required dimensions of an enclosure given the volume..

Fields    
  Volume Enter the desired internal volume of the enclosure. Height, Width, and Depth will change proportionately to give the desired volume.
  Height Enter the internal height of the enclosure. The volume will be recalculated appropriately.
  Width Enter the internal width of the enclosure. The volume will be recalculated appropriately.
  Depth Enter the internal depth of the enclosure. The volume will be recalculated appropriately.
  Ratio Enter the desired height/width or width/depth ratio. When you click “Use this Ratio” the height, width, and depth will be recalculated in the given ratio to produce the desired internal volume.

Results  
  Volume is changed given height, width or depth; or height, width and depth are changed to give a desired volume.
