Measure menu commandsDriver menus

The Measure menu offers the following commands:

Impedance in free air Measure the driver impedance in free air.
Impedance in sealed box Measure the driver impedance in a sealed box.
Impedance Measure the final driver impedance.
Pulse response Measure the pulse response of a driver.
Frequency response Popup a menu with various frequency responses to be measured. See below.

The Frequency response popup submenu offsets the following commands:

On Axis Measure the frequency response on-axis.
30 degrees Measure the frequency response 30 degrees off-axis.
60 degrees Measure the frequency response 60 degrees off-axis.
Nearfield Measure the nearfield frequency response.
Farfield Measure the farfield frequency response.
Port Measure the frequency response of the port.
Gated Measure the gated frequency response of the driver.
