Tuned Calculator Dialog (Options Calculator Tuned)

Use this dialog to calculate resonant frequencies and Q’s of tuned circuits. A tuned circuit is an RLC (resistor, inductor, capacitor) circuit with either all three branches in series or parallel.

Fields Series / Parallel Select series to analyze a series circuit, parallel to analyze a parallel tuned circuit.
  L Enter the inductance of the tuned circuit.
  R Enter the resistance of the tuned circuit.
  C Enter the capacitance of the tuned circuit.

Results Frequency Resonant frequency of the tuned circuit
  Q Q of the tuned circuit.
