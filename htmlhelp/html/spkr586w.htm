Time <> Distance Converter dialog (Options Calculator Distance)

Use this dialog to convert between time, distance and frequency. The frequency is a full-wave for the given time or distance.

Fields    
  Temperature Enter the air temperature in Celsius or Fahrenheit (you can click the unit to change between the two).
  Distance Shows the distance sound would travel to equal an input time. Or shows the wavelength of a frequency.
  Time The time sound would take to travel a given distance, or the time for a full cycle of a given frequency.
  Frequency The frequency which would have a full wavelength equal to a given distance, or which would take a given time to send 1 cycle.

Results  
  The time/distance/frequency are all updated as you change any one of the variables. Change temperature to have the conversion calculation appropriate for a given temperature.
