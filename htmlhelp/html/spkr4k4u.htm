Parameters tab (Driver Properties dialog)

This page contains:

Maximum Excursion The maximum excursion distance of the driver (p-p). This parameter is not currently used.
Motor Strength  
Maximum Power The maximum RMS power the driver can handle. This parameter is not currently used.
Efficiency The output of the driver (in dB at 1 meter) for 1 Watt of input. This parameter is not currently used.
Piston Area The piston area of the driver. This is usually supplied by the manufacturer and not just Pi*R**2.

DC Resistance (Re or Rdc) The DC resistance of the driver.
Mechanical Q (Qms)  
Electrical Q (Qes)  
Total Q (Qts)  
Compliance (Vas) The effective volume of the driver.
Voice Coil inductance (Le) See below
Voice Coil Series (L1) See below
Voice Coil Series (R1) See below
Free Air Resonance (Fs) The free air resonant frequency of the driver.

The driver model is slightly more complex than the traditional driver model. Rather than the usual Le (voice coil inductance) the model uses Le in series with a parallel combination of L1 and R1. These values are shown above.
