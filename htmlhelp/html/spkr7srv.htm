Check Sound Card dialog
(Options Wizard Check Sound Card)

Use this dialog to check out your sound card.

Your sound card is compatible if:

Input Your sound card supports stereo 16 bit input
Output Your sound card supports 16 bit output
Duplex Your sound card supports full duplex operation (simultaneous input and output are available).

Your sound card is fully compatible if also:

Input Your sound card supports 44Khz
Output Your sound card supports 44Khz

If you press the “More” button next to the input or output display, you will get another dialog showing more technical information about your sound card capabilities. This should be self-explanatory.
