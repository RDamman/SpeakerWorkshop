Play Dialog (Sound Play, for Signals)

With this dialog, select the play time, channels, and volume. To play the sound again, without going through this dialog, select the Sound Play Again command.

The type of signal and the sampling rate are selected in the Signal Properties dialog

Fields:    
  Output Volume The output is 0 to 100 as a logarithmic setting (thus 50 is logarithmically ½ of 100).
  Time Duration of the play even. Things like sweeps will automatically fit into the selected time.
  Channels Select left, right, or both. The sound is always mono if both are selected.

Note that the sound mixer in windows can also affect the volume. Use this with caution.
