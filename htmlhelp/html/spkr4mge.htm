Group Delay Dialog (Calculate Group Delay for Datasets)

Group delay can only be calculated on Frequency datasets. It essentially finds the slope of the phase curve at each frequency.

Fields    
  Frequency Range Restrict the frequency range of the calculation by selecting a min and max frequency. When the low frequency information is jittery set the minimum frequency above the jitter.

Click OK to calculate the group delay. This creates a new frequency dataset with a Delay suffix.
